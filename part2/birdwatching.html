Favourite Photos

The Red Cardinal is a small, bright red songbird with a distinct crest and black mask around its beak. It is known for its melodic singing and is commonly found in North and Central America.

A majestic bird of prey, the Bald Eagle is known for its striking white head, dark brown body, and bright yellow beak. This powerful raptor is a symbol of strength and freedom, often associated with courage and independence. Found primarily in North America, it is revered for its keen eyesight and formidable hunting skills, soaring high above forests and lakes in search of its next meal

A vibrant and striking bird, the Peacock is renowned for its dazzling blue-green plumage and the breathtaking tail feathers adorned with intricate eye-like patterns. These magnificent feathers, which it proudly displays during courtship, create a spectacular visual display. Native to South Asia, the peacock is often seen as a symbol of beauty, grace, and elegance, captivating all who witness its grandeur.

The Victoria Crowned Pigeon is a majestic and rare bird, distinguished by its striking vibrant blue feathers and unique lacy crest atop its head. Native to the rainforests of New Guinea, this large and elegant pigeon is further characterized by its vivid red eyes, which contrast beautifully with its plumage. Revered for its grace and beauty, the Victoria Crowned Pigeon is often considered one of the most magnificent pigeons in the world
